The Pilot

Built for
the Field.
Built for
the Frame.

Kyler grew up in southern Indiana — where the woods were a backyard and hunting season was practically a holiday. He graduated from Columbus North High School before heading south to Alabama State University, where he earned his degree and spent four years competing at the collegiate level in baseball.

When his playing days ended, he channeled that same discipline into the air. Kyler taught himself to fly drones and immediately saw what others missed — that this technology wasn't just cool, it was genuinely useful. A thermal camera could find a wounded deer through dense timber. A cinematic camera could transform a wedding into something people watch for the rest of their lives.

From that realization, two businesses were born. Advanced Drone Recovery serves hunters across the region. Advanced Drone Media serves anyone with a story worth capturing from above. One pilot. Two missions. Zero desk jobs.
